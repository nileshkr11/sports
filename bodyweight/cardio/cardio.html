Cardio Exercises for Weight Loss

Cardio workouts are essential for burning calories and improving cardiovascular health. Below are some effective cardio exercises:

Exercise 1: Running

Running is a high-impact cardio exercise that burns a significant amount of calories and strengthens your legs.

Exercise 2: Cycling

Cycling is a low-impact cardio exercise that is great for your heart and helps tone your legs and glutes.

Exercise 3: Jump Rope

Jumping rope is an intense cardio workout that also improves coordination and burns a high number of calories in a short time.
